## 🎮 ANTI-CHEAT MECHANISMS

### **Question Design**
1. **Dynamic Pool System:** Each quiz pulls from 100+ questions, randomized
2. **Scenario Branching:** Wrong answers lead to follow-up questions
3. **Time Pressure:** Questions have appropriate time limits (can't Google everything)
4. **Practical Over Theory:** 60% hands-on exercises, 40% multiple choice
5. **Screenshot Verification:** Must submit proof of work for practical exercises
6. **AI Detection:** Written answers scanned for AI generation
7. **Live Data:** Advanced modules use real blockchain data (can't be predicted)

### **Progression Locks**
- Cannot skip ahead without passing previous module
- Cannot retake same exam within 24 hours
- 3-strike rule (fail 3 times, 7-day lockout)
- Practical exercises require manual review at advanced levels

### **Certification Security**
- Badges are NFTs on blockchain (verifiable, non-transferable)
- Profile displays certification with verification link
- Employers can verify via RMI portal
- Annual recertification required (modules update with new scam types)
