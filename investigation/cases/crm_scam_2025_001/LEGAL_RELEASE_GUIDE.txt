⚠️ WHAT REQUIRES CAUTION
================================================================================

USE CAUTION (Personal Accusations):
-----------------------------------
⚠️ Direct statements: "David Track committed fraud"
⚠️ Criminal labels without conviction: "convicted felon" (unless true)
⚠️ Definitive guilt claims before legal process
⚠️ Accusations of specific crimes without evidence

RISK: Defamation/libel lawsuits from individuals named

SAFER ALTERNATIVE:
Instead of: "David Track is running a scam"
Use: "Blockchain records show wallet activity linked to David Track's 
      admitted statement 'running that scam sosana now too.' 
      Investigation ongoing."

USE CAUTION (Law Enforcement Sensitive):
----------------------------------------
⚠️ Specific FBI case numbers (unless publicly filed)
⚠️ Active investigation details that could tip off suspects
⚠️ Coordination details with law enforcement agencies
⚠️ Pending subpoena or freeze order information

RISK: Compromising active investigation, obstruction concerns

================================================================================
🚨 DO NOT RELEASE (High Risk)
================================================================================

❌ Private communications not already public
❌ Personal contact information of suspects
❌ Allegations about family members not involved
❌ Speculative claims without evidence
❌ Information that could endanger victims
❌ Anything you're not 100% certain is true

🛡️ LEGAL PROTECTIONS FOR YOU
================================================================================

1. TRUTH DEFENSE
   ---------------
   If you publish TRUE statements based on blockchain evidence, you are
   generally protected from defamation claims.
   
   Key: Stick to verifiable blockchain facts

2. PUBLIC RECORDS
   --------------
   Information from:
   • Court filings (public records)
   • SEC enforcement actions (public records)
   • Blockchain transactions (public ledger)
   • Your own screenshots/communications
   
   All generally safe to publish

3. OPINION PROTECTION
   -----------------
   Statements of opinion are generally protected:
   "I believe these patterns suggest systematic fraud"
   "In my opinion, this constitutes market manipulation"

   vs. statements of fact:
   "These people committed fraud" (requires proof)

4. VICTIM PRIVILEGE
   ----------------
   As a victim reporting criminal activity, you have additional protections
   when sharing your experience and evidence.

📝 ATTORNEY CONSULTATION RECOMMENDATION
================================================================================

BEFORE major public release, consider consulting with:

1. DEFAMATION/First Amendment Attorney
   - Review specific statements for legal risk
   - Ensure compliance with state laws
   - Cost: $300-500/hour, 1-2 hours likely sufficient

2. CRIMINAL DEFENSE Attorney (brief consultation)
   - Ensure you're not accidentally compromising investigation
   - Clarify any potential liability concerns
   - Cost: Often free initial consultation

3. VICTIMS' RIGHTS Attorney
   - If organizing victim restitution claims
   - For class action coordination
   - Cost: Often contingency-based

NOT REQUIRED BUT RECOMMENDED:
- First major release (>$15M case)
- If naming specific individuals
- If you have significant personal assets to protect
- If suspects have history of litigation
