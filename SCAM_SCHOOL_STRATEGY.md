# Scam School Strategy: Education as the Growth Engine

## The Core Thesis

**Education is not a side feature — it's the primary growth mechanism.**

Most crypto projects try to acquire users through:
- Token incentives (farmers who dump)
- Paid ads (expensive, low retention)
- Influencer shills (inauthentic, temporary)

RMI Scanner takes a different approach: **become the best free crypto education resource on the internet.** When people learn here, they naturally use the tools. When they use the tools, they discover the token. When they hold the token, they become community members.

### 1. Education Builds Trust
- People are skeptical of crypto projects
- They're NOT skeptical of free education
- By teaching first, we establish authority and goodwill
- When we later suggest tools/token, it feels natural, not salesy

### 2. Education Has Virality
- Someone shares their Scam School cert on Twitter
- "Just learned how honeypots work thanks to @RMIScanner"
- Each graduate becomes a marketer
- Certificates are designed to be shareable

### 3. Education Creates Stickiness
- 30 modules = 30 touchpoints
- Progress tracking = sunk cost fallacy working FOR us
- Gamification (XP, badges, leaderboards) = addiction mechanics
- People return daily to maintain streaks

### 4. Education Justifies the Token
- $CRM isn't just a speculative token
- It's the governance token of an EDUCATION PLATFORM
- Holders vote on new module topics
- Holders get early access to new courses
- Revenue from future premium features flows to token

## The Three Tracks Explained

### Bronze Track (Beginner)
**Target:** People who just bought their first crypto or are about to
**Goal:** Don't lose your funds immediately
**Modules:** Wallets, seed phrases, phishing, Discord safety
**Conversion Point:** "Now that you know how to stay safe, check if your wallet is already compromised → Scanner"

### Silver Track (Intermediate)
**Target:** Active traders, DeFi users, NFT collectors
**Goal:** Protect your gains, spot rugs before they happen
**Modules:** Contract reading, honeypots, liquidity analysis, tokenomics
**Conversion Point:** "Put your new skills to use → Advanced Scanner features (with token utility)"

### Gold Track (Advanced)
**Target:** Aspiring analysts, security researchers, power users
**Goal:** Professional-level blockchain forensics
**Modules:** Wallet clustering, fund tracing, investigation tools
**Conversion Point:** "Join the RMI Investigation Team (token-gated)" + "Staking rewards for contributing research"
